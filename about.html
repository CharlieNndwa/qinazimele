About Us

Qina Zimele Home NPO, founded in 2018, strives to be a haven for senior citizens. They provide a nurturing environment with personalized care plans, prioritizing the well-being of their residents. Their mission extends beyond individual care, aiming to collaborate with others and build a stronger support system for seniors in the community.
Qina Zimele offers personalized care for seniors in a loving home environment. They prioritize resident independence and collaborate to build a stronger community support network.

Our Mission

Our objective is to care for the elderly with frailties and semi-disabled seniors in a loving and respectful manner to ensure their protection and allowing them to express themselves.

Our Vision

Qina Zimele's vision is to accomodate senior citizens and look after the neglected and deserted in the communities.

Our Story

Qina Zimele, Non-Profit Organization

At Qina Zimele Home, we provide a haven for cherished elders in our community who may be feeling alone or lack family support. We treat each resident with dignity, honoring their individuality and right to make choices. Our holistic approach ensures their physical, mental, cultural, and spiritual well-being is nurtured.

Our Mission
