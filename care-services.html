Recognizing that mobility limitations can arise with age, Qina Zimele Home offers comprehensive assistance to ensure your continued independence. Our caring staff is available to provide support with walking, transferring, and navigating our facilities. We also have wheelchairs available for those who require additional assistance. Furthermore, for any medical appointments or hospital visits, our reliable transportation services extend beyond the vehicle. Our staff will accompany you, ensuring a safe and supportive presence throughout the journey, providing you with peace of mind and the ability to focus on your health.

At Qina Zimele Home, we understand that managing chronic health conditions is an important aspect of maintaining well-being. Our staff is trained to support you in effectively managing your chronic conditions. This includes assistance with medication adherence, monitoring vital signs, and keeping track of appointments. We can also help you connect with relevant healthcare professionals and specialists to ensure you receive the most up-to-date treatment plans. Our goal is to empower you to take an active role in managing your health while providing the support you need to live a fulfilling life.

For seniors living with Alzheimer's or dementia, Qina Zimele Home provides a safe and supportive environment that fosters well-being. Our goal is to empower each individual to live life as fully as possible, while providing their families with peace of mind.
